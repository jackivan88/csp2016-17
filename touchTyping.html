Touch Typing

Previous lessons have been based on checkpoints. This lesson will be completed using Google Classroom.

Create An Account

Go to typing.com and sign in with your school Google account. You will be added to my class automatically. Please raise your hand if you cannot complete the signup process.

Complete J, F, and Space

Start working on the first lesson right now. It's extremely important that you position your hands exactly as described in the lessons. Do not look at your hands as you type (even if this slows you down) so that you can learn to type without looking at the keyboard.

Once you finish you will see a screen telling you that you've completed the lesson. Screenshot this and attach it to the "Complete J, F, and Space" Classroom Assignment. If you don't complete it in class, it becomes homework. Future typing lessons will be assigned via Classroom. If you want to work ahead, you may.

Turn in the Classroom assignment for this lesson. You may now work on stretch projects.
